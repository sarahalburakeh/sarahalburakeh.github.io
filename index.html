About

A bit more context

I’ve spent the last decade-plus writing and editing for STEM-focused audiences – with a particular love for stories where engineering, climate, infrastructure, and everyday life collide.

As Strategic Content Editor at ASME, I work across features, digital products, podcasts, and sponsored content to keep engineering stories sharp, accurate, and engaging. I’m especially interested in how editorial teams can better serve early-career engineers and students while still speaking to seasoned experts.

Before ASME, I worked with startups and organizations where I collaborated closely with internal and external teams, led editorial efforts, and learned how to build processes in fast-moving, resource-constrained environments.

Outside of work, you’ll usually find me deep in a training cycle, exploring new cities, or scheming up better ways to make engineering-focused content feel less like homework and more like something you’d actually choose to read.
